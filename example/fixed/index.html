We are really excited to announce our 8th Meetup on Monday 6th November at HomeAway, London from 6:30pm onwards.


https://goo.gl/maps/deynRbb4hzw

• Joel Almeida, Software Engineer @ HomeAway - During November’s BYOP Joel from HomeAway will take to the floor to talk us through, “What if we just reduced page size?”

• Dominic Adams, Software Engineering Lead @ Lloyds Banking Group - Having developed and published a React Native game, Dom will show how Fastlane can be used to automate deployments to the Apple App Store.

Would you like to speak? Please get in touch with James over Twitter at @mcleo_d.

Refreshments


Pizzas, salads, beers and soft drinks

Things to note

  • A laptop and desire to explore React is recommended

  • Please RSVP to secure a place

  • All levels are welcome
